## 🧪 Security: What You Need to Know

Unlike Roblox executors, 1Password is a legitimate, highly respected security tool. However, new users often ask **is 1Password safe for Mac** or **can 1Password be hacked**. Here are the facts:

- **No service is 100% immune.** 1Password has never been breached in its history, but your master password remains your ultimate responsibility.
- **Encryption is local.** Your data is encrypted on your device using your master password + a secret key. AgileBits cannot access your vault even if compelled by law.
- **Phishing remains a risk.** Even with 1Password, you must still verify website URLs before autofilling. The browser extension helps by refusing to fill on lookalike domains.

To reduce risk:

- Choose a long, memorable, unique master password (e.g., 4‑5 random words).
- Enable two‑factor authentication (2FA) on your 1Password account itself.
- Download **1Password for Mac** only from our Release pages.
- Keep your macOS and 1Password app updated for the latest security patches.
